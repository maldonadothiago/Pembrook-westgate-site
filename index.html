About Pembrook & Westgate

Located in Gurnee, Illinois, the Pembrook & Westgate neighborhoods are known for their tree-lined streets, spacious homes, excellent schools, and a strong sense of community.

With quick access to I-94, Gurnee Mills, Great Wolf Lodge, and top-rated restaurants, it’s an ideal location for families and professionals alike.
